## PRODUCTION TIPS

1. **Screen recording:** Use your live site at club.coachmode.ai. Record at 1920x1080.
2. **Cursor:** Use a smooth cursor tool (like Screenflow or ScreenStudio) for polished mouse movements.
3. **Transitions:** Quick dissolves between sections. No flashy transitions.
4. **Text overlays:** Use the same Inter font and lime green (#D3FB52) on dark navy (#002838).
5. **AI editing:** Paste this script into invideo.ai or Descript, upload your screen recordings, and it will auto-edit.
6. **Thumbnail:** Dark navy background, lime "ClubMode AI" text, "6 Tools. One Platform." subtitle, tennis/pickleball imagery.
